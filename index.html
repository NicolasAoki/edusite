Annotation and distribution of ncRNA families in genomes of Streptococcus agalactiae*

Research

Research studies have shown that non-coding RNAs (ncRNAs) can act modulating gene expression in bacteria, as well as coordinating adaptive processes that respond to environmental changes and control the expression of target genes

ncRNA

Here we report an ncRNA distribution analysis over more than five thousand genomic regions for of S. agalactiae.
